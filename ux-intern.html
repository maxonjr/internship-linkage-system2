About the Internship

Join our design team to create intuitive and beautiful user experiences for web and mobile applications. You'll participate in the entire design process from research to prototyping.

Responsibilities

  • Conduct user research and analyze results
  • Create user flows, wireframes, and prototypes
  • Design user interfaces in Figma or Adobe XD
  • Participate in usability testing sessions
  • Collaborate with developers to implement designs

Requirements

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Design, HCI, or related field
  • Basic understanding of UX principles and methodologies
  • Familiarity with design tools (Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch)
  • Portfolio demonstrating design thinking
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills

Nice to Have

  • Experience with user research methods
  • Knowledge of HTML/CSS
  • Understanding of accessibility standards
  • Motion design skills

What We Offer

  • Hands-on experience with real client projects
  • Mentorship from senior designers
  • Flexible work schedule
  • Opportunity to build your professional portfolio
  • Potential for full-time employment after graduation

About Digital Creations

Digital Creations is a boutique design agency specializing in user experience and interface design. We work with startups and established companies to create digital products that users love.
